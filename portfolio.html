            Design Process

            From use context to a refined physical object.

            The process is deliberately visible because recruiters need to understand design judgment, not only the final render.

            1. 01Research

              Map users, scenes, constraints, and the reason a product should exist.

            2. 02Sketch

              Explore proportions, gestures, silhouettes, and product families quickly.

            3. 03Prototype

              Use paper, foam, mockups, or 3D tests to make scale and ergonomics visible.

            4. 04Test

              Compare handling, stability, assembly logic, and user clarity before refinement.

            5. 05Refine

              Tune CMF, part lines, radii, details, and presentation hierarchy.

            6. 06Present

              Build a clear design story from scenario to final physical form.
